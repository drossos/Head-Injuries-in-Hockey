Concussions in Hockey

Mission Statement

This website is dedicated to raise awarness of head injuries within ice hockey as well as brining public attention to the disease Chronic Traumatic Encphalopathy (CTE). Hopefully through education and awarness hockey will become a more safer game encouraging more to play.

Head Injuries and Hockey

Hockey is a fast paced full contact support where injuries are frequent and expected. The evoloution of head protection in hockey has been substantial going from no helmets at all to advanced head protection desigined to reduce impact and damages taken by the head. Despite these advances, debilitating head injuries are suffered by hockey players of all skill levels every year.
